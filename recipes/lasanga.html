Our Lasanga Dish

Lasagna just rocks. Full stop. And if you’ve never tried a homemade one before, that needs to change! If you can make spaghetti bolognese, you can make lasagne. It just requires a wee bit more patience. OK, bit more than a wee bit more patience. But it’s totally worth it. A real homemade Lasagna is epic.

ingredients

  • Onion, garlic, carrot and celery – for the flavour base, a soffrito
  • Beef
  • Seasonings – beef bouillon cubes (stock cubes), bay leaves, thyme, oregano, Worcestershire sauce
  • Red wine – for extra flavour!
